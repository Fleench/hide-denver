# How the App Works

## Mission Area
Stay inside the clear active polygon shown on the map. The area outside the active polygon is heavily dimmed and is out of bounds.

## Out-of-Bounds Warning
If your live position leaves the active mission area, the app shows a full-screen warning. Return to the mission area immediately.

## Testing Override
During local testing only, the warning may be dismissed with **I am testing the app ignore this message**. This does not change the mission boundary.

## Pin Shrink
Touch-and-hold or mouse-hold on the map to drop a pin. The app creates a 1/4-mile radius around that pin and intersects it with the original mission polygon.

## Saved Pin
The active pin is saved in this browser. Reloading the page restores the saved pin and shrunken mission area.

## Reset Map
Use **Reset Map** to clear the saved pin and return the map to the original mission boundary.

## Location Tracking
The app uses browser geolocation while the page is visible. Tracking pauses when the page is hidden or closed.

# Game Rules

## Allowed RTD Lines
All RTD rail lines are allowed except the **A line**.

Allowed bus lines are: `15`, `15L`, `3`, `0`, `0L`, `6`, `10`, `20`, `32`, `38`, `43`, `31`, `40`, `51`, `24`, `73`, `76`, `105`, `FREE`, `FMR`, `83D`, `83L`, `120X`, `FF1`.

## Stay in the Active Zone
Players must remain inside the clear active polygon during play unless the game master says otherwise.

## Follow Pin Changes
When a game master drops a pin, the active area shrinks to the 1/4-mile pin radius clipped against the original mission boundary.

## Return Immediately
If the out-of-bounds warning appears during real play, return to the active mission area immediately.

## Game Master Authority
The game master may pause play, clear the pin, reset the map, or clarify boundaries at any time.
